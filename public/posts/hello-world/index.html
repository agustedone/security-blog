N8N and three CVEs on its way

July 31, 2025 · 1 min · Security Research Team

n8n is basically Lego for automation: you snap little blocks — called nodes — together to make something cool for you or your team. There are trigger nodes, action nodes, logic nodes, you name it. Chain them up and voilà: “when someone clicks, hit this API; if the response is 200 OK, drop a message in my Telegram.”

This takes a ton of grunt work off dev teams and even lets non-coders get things done.

But heads-up: mis-configure it or ignore security best practices and n8n turns into a double-edged sword.
